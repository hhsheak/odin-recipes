Shiozake

Description

A quick and simple meal for a light breakfast, here is a simple recipe to whip up a plate of delicious shiozake!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Salt
  • Sake

Steps

  1. Marinate salmon with sake and let it rest
  2. Chill in freezer for 2 days
  3. Grill for 3 minutes
